about
/001

Edgar Weltens aka Wave is a Dutch music producer and performer based in the south of Spain. In his earlier years Wave spent a lot of time exploring different genres and perfecting his craft as a producer.

Eventually he had to come out of the studio and take his music to the stage, has he did not want to follow the route of most producers and become a DJ, he looked for other techniques to perform his music and offer a more organic live alternative.

After some investigating he decided to go for a technique called Live Looping.
